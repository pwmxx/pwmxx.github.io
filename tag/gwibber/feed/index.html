Wordpress SEO Expert https://peterwm.com/ Peter Warwick-Mahoney Mon, 08 Jul 2013 15:08:51 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=5.4.2 Gwibber and the new Twitter API https://peterwm.com/gwibber-and-the-new-twitter-api/ Mon, 08 Jul 2013 15:08:51 +0000 http://www.petermahoney.me/?p=862 [Continue Reading]]]> I noticed Gwibber, my social networking app for Ubuntu, stopped updating my Twitter status after the API was updated. Basically, it was broken.

And as of yet, it’s not been patched.

Here’s a fix I used to get Ubuntu updating my Twitter account again.

[sourcecode]pkill gwibber
sudo gedit "/usr/share/gwibber/plugins/twitter/__init__.py"[/sourcecode]

Then search and replace “api.twitter.com/1” to “api.twitter.com/1.1”

Tada!

For the sake of honesty, I’ll admit I didn’t find this fix myself, I found it here.
