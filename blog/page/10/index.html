    This is just a trick to get all the unnecessary spaces and characters out of your code. It‚Äôs particularly useful for javascript and CSS, but your output HTML is worth shrinking too.   5. Javascript at the bottom.
    Place all the Javascript you can at the bottom of your page‚Äôs code. Again, it‚Äôs a speed thing.
